ABOUT US

MakeMyTrip

One of India's leading online travel agencies, MakeMyTrip offers a wide range of travel services, including flight bookings, hotel reservations, holiday packages, and car rentals. The platform provides users with various options to plan and customize their trips. .

Goibibo

Goibibo is a comprehensive travel platform that not only facilitates flight and hotel bookings but also offers bus and train ticket reservations. Known for its user-friendly interface, Goibibo often provides discounts, deals, and cashback offers to users.

Cleartrip

Cleartrip is a travel portal that emphasizes simplicity and ease of use. It allows users to book flights, hotels, and trains seamlessly. The platform is known for its transparent pricing and straightforward booking process.

Yatra

Yatra is a full-service travel portal that covers flights, hotels, trains, and holiday packages. It provides a range of options for travelers, including international and domestic destinations. Yatra is also recognized for its customer support services.

Ixigo

Ixigo is a travel search engine that aggregates and compares prices from various travel websites. It helps users find the best deals on flights, hotels, buses, and trains. Ixigo also provides useful information such as fare predictions and train running status.

RedBus

Specializing in bus ticket bookings, RedBus is a widely used platform for travelers in India. In addition to bus services, RedBus also offers hotel booking options, making it a convenient choice for those planning both their transportation and accommodation.

Trivago

Trivago is a hotel search engine that allows users to compare prices from various hotel booking sites. It simplifies the process of finding the best hotel deals by presenting a comprehensive list of options and their respective prices.

IRCTC

As the official platform for Indian Railways, IRCTC enables users to book train tickets, check train schedules, and manage reservations. It plays a crucial role in facilitating train travel across the country.

Air India

The official website for Air India is the go-to platform for booking both domestic and international flights with India's national carrier. Users can access information on flight schedules, manage bookings, and explore special offers provided by Air India.

OYO

Initially known for its hotel booking services, OYO has expanded to offer a variety of travel-related services. In addition to budget and premium hotel options, OYO provides vacation packages, homestays, and experiences, making it a one-stop solution for travelers.
